Wall Workflow

Pull a stack off the wall, spread it open, rotate through the contents, and throw it back.

This is a touch-first wall interface for catalogs, media reels, folders, binders, and field packets. The controls are deliberately oversized and tuned for swipe, throw, fan-open, and page-turn interactions.
